Noodles with tomato-sauce

noddles with Tomatosauce

Description

This is a simple recipe for pasta with tomato sauce. It is quick, easy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 500g strained tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp yeast flakes
  • 1 tbsp garden herbs
